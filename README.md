# PaperForge PMC

PaperForge PMC is a place where I mess around with mods, ideas, and experiments for Paper Minecraft.

It’s basically a collection of stuff I’ve made (and sometimes other people have made too), including random concepts, small tweaks, and things I wanted to try out.

## What this is

This isn’t a finished modpack or anything polished. It’s more of a sandbox where I can:

- Test ideas
- Make small mods or changes
- Try random concepts
- See what can be done in Paper Minecraft

## What you’ll find

- Experimental mods  
- Half-finished ideas  
- Small gameplay changes  
- Random builds or concepts  
- Stuff that might break or not make sense

## Why it exists

Just for fun and experimentation. No big goal, just seeing what works and what doesn’t inside Paper Minecraft.

## Note

Most of this is unfinished or experimental, so expect weird or broken things.
