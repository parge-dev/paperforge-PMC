About

PaperForge PMC is a place where people and i can mess around with mods and ideas for Paper Minecraft. It’s basically a gallery of stuff people and i made, plus random concepts and experiments.

The binary thing is there because cloud variables only use numbers, so mod ideas are sent using 1s and 0s. It’s a bit weird, but it works.

Mostly this is just about trying new things, making cool stuff, and seeing what Paper Minecraft can do.
